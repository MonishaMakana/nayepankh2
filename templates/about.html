About Nayepankh Foundation

Building communities through volunteer service and social impact

Our Mission

Nayepankh Foundation is dedicated to harnessing the power of volunteerism to create positive social change. We believe that everyone has the ability to make a difference in their community.

Our Vision

To build a society where volunteering is recognized as a key vehicle for personal development, social cohesion, and the achievement of the Sustainable Development Goals.

What We Do

  • Connect Volunteers - Match passionate individuals with meaningful opportunities
  • Develop Skills - Provide training and mentorship for volunteer growth
  • Create Impact - Measure and showcase the difference volunteers make
  • Build Community - Foster connections and mutual support among volunteers

Our Values

Compassion
We care deeply about the communities we serve

Integrity
We act with honesty and transparency

Inclusivity
We welcome everyone regardless of background

Impact
We measure and maximize our positive influence
